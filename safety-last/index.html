Safety Last!
Original Score

An original orchestral score composed for one of cinema's greatest silent films — Harold Lloyd's Safety Last! (1923). A unique film-concert event that unites cinematic heritage with contemporary orchestral creation.

Promotional video — original score synchronized with film fragments. Score and all parts are ready for immediate performance.

A score that respects the tradition — and transcends it

The music is conceived to enhance the film's breathless pace, physical comedy, and moments of genuine tension. It combines a language respectful of silent cinema tradition with modern, expressive orchestral writing accessible to all audiences.

✦
Performance-ready. Full score and all orchestral parts are prepared and available for immediate use.
✦
No click track required. Rhythmic structure and synchronization points are built into the musical writing itself.
✦
New audiences. A singular event that connects cinematic heritage with contemporary composition — ideal for family, crossover and film-concert programming.
✦
Composer-conductor on the podium. Daniel Sánchez Velasco is available to conduct the premiere and provide all preparatory sessions.
